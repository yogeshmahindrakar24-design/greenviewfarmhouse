About Our Farm House

Green View Farm House is a peaceful getaway surrounded by nature, offering a perfect escape from the busy city life of Mumbai. Located in the beautiful and green surroundings of Goregaon East, our farm house is designed for people who want to relax, celebrate special moments, and spend quality time with family and friends.

Whether you are planning a birthday party, a family gathering, a small celebration, or a weekend break, Green View Farm House provides a calm and refreshing environment. The property is surrounded by greenery, open space, and fresh air, giving you a natural and relaxing experience away from the noise and stress of the city.

Our farm house is ideal for day outings, private parties, couple getaways, and group celebrations. Guests can enjoy a peaceful atmosphere, beautiful views, and a comfortable space where they can relax, celebrate, and create memorable moments.

At Green View Farm House, we focus on providing a simple, clean, and enjoyable place where people can reconnect with nature and spend quality time together. It is the perfect destination for relaxation, celebrations, and unforgettable memories with your loved ones.
